Our Story

Andrea

I’m Andrea, a recycler of rubbish and founder of MTNestCrafts, a small eco-focused business. I spend my time turning waste materials into beautiful gifts that have a use in the home.

Back in 2010 I rescued a pile of shot glasses from the bin at a local cider festival. That was the beginning of my creative recycling journey. Influenced by the huge lanterns hanging in the trees at my favourite festival, I set about making miniature versions for my daughter’s campervan.

They were a hit with friends who encouraged me to make more. Fast forward to 2016 — due to debilitating migraines — I retired from working as a TA in my local school and started my own small business turning waste materials into lovely gifts.

This allows me to work when I am feeling well and not to feel so guilty when I can't. Fast forward to the present day and I am still here, making unique, vintage-style lampshade fairy lights and bottle lights — repurposing rubbish and saving plastic from landfill.
